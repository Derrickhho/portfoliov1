Educational Social Platform

This project is a concept for an educational platform that establishes better communication between students and teachers outside of the classroom. This includes intuitive and easy access to information students often need without the clutter of many other traditional education platforms.

Concept Idea

My idea behind this project came from a frustration with other educational platforms used in high school and college; moodle.com and blackboard.edu respectively. The main problem I noticed with these websites was the confusing and often inefficient navigation. This meant that it often took an inefficient amount of clicks to just find a homework assignment due date or course syllabus. Along with the click-count, the clutter of different page layouts was a communication block between teachers and students.

The main reasoning behind this clutter was the over-flexibility that teachers had of customizing their own page navigation. This made it difficult to get used to an interface or learn to use it.

In addition, I found myself and other students communicating by means of Facebook or text asking what an assignment was or when it was due. This concept incorporates a way for students to communicate with one another and additionally their teachers.
